Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Arion VPN (the "App"), developed by Kzova Labs. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy details what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data when you use our service.

2. Strict No-Logs Policy

We are firmly committed to a strict no-logs policy. When you use the Arion VPN service, we DO NOT collect, store, or share any of the following data:

  • Browsing history or websites you visit
  • DNS queries
  • Your actual IP address or the VPN IP address assigned to you
  • Connection timestamps or session durations
  • The content of your data traffic

Because we do not collect this information, we cannot share it with any third parties, including government entities.

3. Android VpnService API Usage

Arion VPN uses the Android VpnService API as its core functionality. The VpnService is used exclusively to create a secure, device-level encrypted tunnel to our relay servers. This encrypts your traffic to protect it from local network interception. The traffic routed through the VpnService is not logged, inspected, or recorded by us.

4. Advertising and Analytics

Our app is provided for free and is supported by advertisements. We use Google AdMob to display ads.

To serve ads and analyze app performance, our advertising partners (like Google) may collect anonymized, non-personally identifiable information, including:

  • Advertising ID (Google Play Services ID)
  • Device model and OS version
  • Approximate geographical location (country level) for relevant ad serving

You may opt-out of personalized advertising through your Android device's privacy settings or via the GDPR/CCPA consent form presented when you open the app (if applicable in your region).

5. Third-Party Servers (SoftEther/VPNGate)

Arion VPN routes your traffic through volunteer-operated relay servers provided by the SoftEther VPN / VPNGate academic project. While we enforce strict encryption, we do not own or manage these volunteer servers. Please refer to the VPNGate project policies regarding their network operations.
