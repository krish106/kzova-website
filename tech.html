Smart routing and Windows software without making products heavy.

Architecture

The backend protects both users and the app.

Arion VPN should not make every app user fetch public VPN lists directly. The backend caches, filters, scores, and serves cleaner route choices.

Fetch and cache

The backend refreshes server data on a controlled schedule and keeps a last-good cache for resilience.

Filter and score

Very high ping, weak speed, low uptime, and recently failed servers can be excluded or temporarily penalized.

Serve app-friendly routes

The Android app receives cleaner route options and can retry the next best candidate when a connection fails.
